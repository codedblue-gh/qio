druk wide

This is a strong sans serif font family, with solid lines, paired with bold yet soft rounding.

It’s perfect for large headlines which we’ve actively used in this specific project

Suisse Int'l

Suisse Int’l is a neo-grotesque sans-serif typeface, which combines classic style with cutting edge design quality.

It creates a perfect contrast with our head font
