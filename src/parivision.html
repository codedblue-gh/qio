typography

The combination of Suisse Int’l and Heading Now strikes a balance between clarity and intensity. Suisse Int’l provides a solid, readable foundation for all textual content, while Heading Now injects power and personality into key visuals. Together, they reinforce the team's modern aesthetic and professional tone, while also delivering the bold attitude that defines the world of esports.

Suisse Int’l

Suisse Int’l is a clean, modern sans-serif typeface used for body text. It offers high legibility across devices and screen sizes, making it perfect for conveying information clearly and efficiently. With its neutral tone and refined proportions.

Suisse Int’l supports the fast-paced, tech-forward identity of an esports organization.

Heading Now

Heading Now is a bold, impactful typeface designed for headlines. Its geometric structure and strong presence help create a distinctive, energetic visual language.

Ideal for titles, player names, and key moments, Heading Now captures attention and reflects the competitive spirit of esports.
