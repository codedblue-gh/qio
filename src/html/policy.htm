  • Terminology

    • Personal Data: Any information relating directly or indirectly to an identified or identifiable natural person (subject of personal data).

    • Processing of Personal Data: Any action or set of actions performed with or without the use of automated tools on personal data. This includes collection, recording, systematization, accumulation, storage, clarification (updating, modification), retrieval, usage, transfer (distribution, provision, access), anonymization, blocking, deletion, and destruction of personal data.

    • Automated Processing of Personal Data: Processing personal data using computer technology.

    • Dissemination of Personal Data: Actions aimed at disclosing personal data to an indefinite number of persons.

    • Providing Personal Data: Actions aimed at disclosing personal data to a specific person or a defined group of persons.

    • Blocking Personal Data: Suspension of personal data processing (unless the processing is necessary to clarify personal data).

    • Use of Personal Data: Actions (operations) with personal data performed by qio to make decisions or perform other actions that result in legal consequences concerning the subject of personal data or other persons, or otherwise affect the rights and freedoms of the subject of personal data or other persons.

    • Destruction of Personal Data: Actions resulting in the irreversible elimination of personal data content in the information system and/or the destruction of the physical carriers of personal data.

    • Site: A collection of information, texts, graphic elements, design, images, photos, video materials, and other intellectual property, as well as computer programs, contained in an information system that ensures the availability of such information on the Internet at the address qio.me.

    • Cookies: Data automatically transmitted to qio during the use of the Site via the software installed on the User's device. This includes IP address, geographic location, browser information, type of operating system, technical characteristics of the equipment and software used by the User, and the date and time of access to the Site. Cookies can be permanent (persistent cookies) and remain on the user's device until deleted or temporary (session cookies), which are stored only until the browser is closed. Additionally, cookies are classified as primary (set directly by the visited Site) and third-party (set by other sites).

    • IP Address: A numeric label used by computers on a network to identify the computer each time they access the Internet.

  • Use of Cookies

    qio Studio is committed to ensuring that your experience on our Site is as comfortable as possible. To achieve this, we analyze user behavior, preferences, and interests using cookies. This analysis helps us improve your interaction with the Site, determine the most user-friendly interface, and enhance navigation.

    According to the International Chamber of Commerce classification, qio Studio uses the following categories of cookies:

    • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for navigating the Site and using certain services, such as accessing secure pages, registering, and logging in. They also remember your actions on previous pages during the same session.

    • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect aggregated information about how the Site is used. The data, stored on the user's device between browser sessions, includes metrics such as time spent on the Site, most frequently visited pages, and the effectiveness of advertising and marketing campaigns. The information collected is used for statistical and analytical purposes and may be shared with third parties authorized by the web resource.

    • Functional Cookies: Functional cookies save settings or configurations stored on the user's device between browser sessions. This includes information like username, profile photo, comments, site language, and location. These cookies also enable users to watch videos, participate in interactive activities, and interact with social networks. Some data may be shared with third parties authorized by the web resource.

    • Targeting Cookies: Targeting cookies provide content that may be of interest to the user, stored on the user's device between browser sessions. This includes tracking recommended text, graphics, audio, and video material, managing targeted advertising, evaluating advertising campaign effectiveness, and tracking user visits to other resources. This information may be shared with other parties, such as media clients, advertisers, agencies, and business partners, to provide quality targeted advertising.

    qio Studio ensures that all information collected through cookies is used to enhance your experience and improve our services. We adhere to strict privacy and security measures to protect your data.
