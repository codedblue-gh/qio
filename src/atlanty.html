typography

Combining Futura and Circe Contrast creates a visually striking and harmonious typographic design that balances modernity with elegance. Such combination offers a unique blend of modernity and tradition

Futura

Futura is known for its clean, modern lines and its emphasis on geometric shapes, particularly circles, triangles, and squares.

Its aesthetic appeal and functional design makes it a perfect primary font to use

Circe Contrast

Circe Contrast is a stylish and versatile typeface that combines traditional serif characteristics with modern design elements

Despite its decorative qualities, Circe Contrast maintains a high level of legibility, making it suitable for both display and text use. The clear letterforms ensure that it can be easily read at various sizes.
